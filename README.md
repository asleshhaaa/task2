Task 2 – Phishing Email Analysis

# Overview
This task involved analyzing a suspicious email to identify phishing indicators and understand how attackers use spoofing and social engineering techniques.

# Email Summary
The email claimed that an SBI account would be suspended within 24 hours unless verification was completed through a provided link.
Sender: support@sbi-security-alerts.com

 #Key Phishing Indicators
Domain Mismatch: sbi-security-alerts.com ≠ official sbi.co.in
Urgent Language: “Suspended within 24 hours”, “Verify immediately”
Suspicious URL: http://sbi-verification-update.ru/login
 (.ru domain)
Generic Greeting: “Dear Customer”
Suspicious Attachment: Account_Verification_Form.zip
Header Failures: SPF, DKIM, and DMARC failed

#Tools Used
Sample phishing email
Online email header analyzer

#Conclusion
Multiple red flags confirm that the email is a phishing attempt intended to steal sensitive banking information.
